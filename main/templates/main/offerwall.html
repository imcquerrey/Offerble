Frequently Asked Questions

How Offerble Rewards Works

What is Offerble Rewards?

Offerble Rewards allows you to earn free credits in your favorite game or website by participating in advertising promotions. Choose an offer that appeals most to you and follow the instructions to receive your free credits.

Can I complete an offer more than once?

Offers from our advertisers can only be completed once per household unless otherwise specified.

When will I receive credits?

Each offer will take a different amount of time to credit. The amount of time required for a credit will be stated in the description for the offer.

Offer Status on Support page

'Offer Viewed' status

'Offer Viewed' means that you have clicked on an offer, but we are waiting for the advertiser to verify the completion. If you believe that you have completed an offer correctly, but it has not credited, please contact support.

'Cancelled' status

'Cancelled' means that the offer was originally verified as complete, but the advertiser later decided that this completion was not valid. This can happen if you did not follow the instructions, meet certain requirements, or entered valid information. Unfortunately, this status can not be changed.

Problems

I haven't received credit for an offer.

You should check whether the offer shows up in your offer history and whether you have waited the required amount of time for completion. If it does, you can contact support using the link on the Support page.

The offer leads to an incorrect or blank page.

This is likely due to a technical issue with the advertiser. Please report this issue to us via the 'Contact' link on the Support page.
